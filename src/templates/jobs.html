Join the PaperHive team

PaperHive is a software company in the field of scientific research and collaboration. After becoming a part of the Centre for Entrepreneurship incubator at the TU Berlin and receiving the EXIST startup grant, the company is releasing its product in early 2016.

PaperHive is the coworking hub for researchers, aiming to support knowledge-creators in their work and to dramatically increase open collaboration in science.

We are looking for incredibly creative students willing to develop their practical skills in marketing, event management, and media relations. We strive for high diversity in our team (learn about us here) and would love to welcome people from all over the world with various study and work backgrounds as our colleagues.

PR and media intern

As a PR and media intern you will have the chance to use your entire creativity to establish PaperHive’s presence in media worldwide.

You can pick from several challenging tasks that we want to tackle together with you:

  • Support the planning and implementation of market entry strategies in different international markets and/or scientific fields.
  • Conception and autonomous implementation of projects in the fields of media relations, content marketing, and brand management.

Community building and event management intern

As a marketing intern in community building and event management you will help develop PaperHive’s community of researchers in Berlin.

Here are a few examples of tasks that we would like to accomplish with you:

  • Meeting and interviewing Berlin scientists and introducing PaperHive to them.
  • Conception and autonomous organization of great events for and with great scientists.
  • Attending scientific events and representing PaperHive.

Social media intern

As a social media intern you will help develop PaperHive's presence online building a strong and active followership of the brand.

Here are a few examples of tasks that we would like to accomplish with you:

  • Helping with the management of PaperHive’s facebook and twitter accounts
  • Planning and implementing creative growth campaigns in social media
  • Establishing social media partnerships

Your profile

At PaperHive, we need three team players to join our team in the end of March who

  • Your studies are ideally in media, publishing, business, marketing, or another related field.
  • You don’t fear tasks like talking to a professor about a Berlin startup that improves his/her workflow.
  • You have experience managing a facebook fan-page or a twitter account.
  • You don’t fear tasks like interviewing a Nobel Prize Laureate or convincing Silicon Valley news websites to write about a Berlin startup.
  • The texts you write get your readers thinking and acting.
  • speak English or German fluently

Our offer

  • Grow: During your internship you will learn to apply the principles of lean marketing, growth hacking and guerilla marketing techniques, get hands-on experience with social media campaigns, blogging, media relations, event management and brand strategy.
  • Team spirit: Work in a small, dynamic, and open-minded team that is driven by the enthusiasm for a cause with a global impact.
  • Openness and flexibility: We are always open to progressive ideas in any direction.
  • Entrepreneurial workplace: Experience the dynamic nature of a young startup based in Mitte (near Nordbahnhof, in the TU Gründungsinsel). Flora Mate and finest Italian espresso await you (if you’re into caffeine). We also cook delicious pasta.
  • Appreciation and respect: Receive an official certificate for your internship (Pflichtpraktikumsbescheinigung) valid in front of your university authorities as well as references for future job applications.
