Join the PaperHive team

PaperHive is a software company in the field of scientific research and collaboration. After becoming a part of the Centre for Entrepreneurship incubator at the TU Berlin and receiving the EXIST startup grant, the company is releasing its product in early 2016.

PaperHive is the coworking hub for researchers, aiming to support knowledge-creators in their work and to dramatically increase open collaboration in science.

The Berlin-based startup-team consists of André and Nico (mathematicians, computer scientists, free-software and open-access enthusiasts) and Alexander (social entrepreneur, educator, business developer). We strive for high diversity in our team and encourage you to apply regardless of your (and our) identity.

Junior backend developer Node.js, MongoDB

As a backend developer you will extend the PaperHive REST API with us. We use Node.js 5 with fresh ES2015 features that make asynchronous programming fun again – say goodbye to the callback hell and welcome generators and the yield keyword! Our middleware is koa (a better express.js) and our database is MongoDB.

You can pick from several challenging tasks that we want to tackle together with you:

  • efficient import/update/backup strategies of large datasets
  • maintaining versioned documents for guaranteed citability
  • full-text search integration with elasticsearch
  • real-time updates via websockets
  • transactions in a distributed database
  • improve server infrastructure
  • {{insert your idea here}}

Junior frontend developer AngularJS

If you are a frontend developer you will have fun improving our AngularJS-based HTML5-frontend with our team. We like clean and testable code that is split into reusable directives that are released as free software (see our Github profile).

Here are a few examples of tasks that we would like to accomplish with you:

  • improve our PDF directive with efficient rendering through PDF.js
  • develop rich filters for discussions
  • optimize UI workflow for usability
  • beautify discussion presentation with less/sass

Qualifications

At PaperHive, we need two team players to join our team in mid January who

  • strive to learn and apply new concepts every day
  • have 1-2 years of development experience with modern JavaScript (ES2015 is not a requirement, you can learn it on the go)
  • have backend or frontend experience (both would also be great!)
  • build reusable code with accompanying unit tests
  • speak English or German fluently

Knowledge of modern JavaScript frameworks is a plus (e.g., AngularJS for frontend and Node.js for backend).

Our offer

  • Create: Build innovative software using the frameworks of tomorrow.
  • Grow: Learn from software engineers with a combined experience of more than 30 years.
  • Team spirit: Work in a small, dynamic, and open-minded team that is driven by the enthusiasm for a cause with a global impact.
  • Openness and flexibility: We are always open to progressive ideas in any direction. We’re not afraid of changing our codebase significantly if it pays off.
  • Test-driven development: Code is only merged when unit tests and end-to-end tests pass.
  • Entrepreneurial workplace: Experience the dynamic nature of a young startup based in Mitte (near Nordbahnhof, in the TU Gründungsinsel). Flora Mate and finest Italian espresso await you (if you’re into caffeine). We also cook delicious pasta.
  • Appreciation and respect: Receive a fair salary for your work and a proper recognition for your results. Work part-time (40-80 hours per month) and enjoy a flexible working time.
