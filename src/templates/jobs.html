About PaperHive

PaperHive has been developed for several months now and is fairly close to a preliminary release. We're currently reaching out for the EXIST-Gründerstipendium to ensure basic funding for the next 12 months. The EXIST grant comprises 2500-3000 EUR/month per co-founder (depending on academic degree).

Our founding team is based in Berlin and consists of André and Nico (mathematicians, computer scientists, free software and open access enthusiasts) who developed the current prototype. Several other people help with web design and server management. We strive for high diversity in our team and encourage you to apply regardless of your (and our) identity.

Co-founder, business executive

Business relations and management in scientific publishing, development and implementation of market entrance strategy.

Job description

Together with us, you will develop and implement a strategy for PaperHive's market entrance. Furthermore, you will be responsible for the maintenance of customer relations with scientific publishers as well as business administration and management all around PaperHive. This includes sales and marketing but since we're a small team, other interesting tasks may arise along the way. Bringing in your own ideas is most important to us.

Qualifications

You have work experience in scientific publishing and maintain good contacts into the business. Since we are situated in the startup ecosystem, it'd be of great value for us if you have experience in this field. At PaperHive, we need

  • work experience in scientific publishing
  • business experience: sales, marketing, customer relations
  • startup spirit and experience
  • presentation and communication skills

We also welcome

  • project management experience

Our offer

  • work in a small, dynamic, and open-minded team that is driven by enthusiasm for the idea
  • be part of a movement that makes research fun again
  • experience all the ups and downs of a startup endeavor
  • your opinion counts: help steering the ship from day 1 on
  • flexible working time
  • 2500-3000 EUR for 1 year (if the EXIST application succeeds)

Application details

Please email us (info@paperhive.org) your CV and links to projects you've been involved with. We don't need a cover letter.
