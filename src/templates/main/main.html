Discuss and learn

Is something unclear in an article? Ask a question and have it answered by experts. Discuss interesting points with others and clarify the complex bits. You think you've found a mistake? Post it on PaperHive and let others profit from your insight – just as you profit from the insight of others. Papers are understood better together.

About PaperHive

Everything in one place

"Is this your homework, Larry?" No more messing around with comments across your desk. All your annotations in one place, accessible from everywhere. With infinite space in the margin.

Comments with style

Use mathematics, source code, markup, and more to structure your annotations and discussions. No more need to squint your eyes: Everything is instantlyrendered in the browser. Beautiful!

Discussion threads

If a question spins off to be a larger discussion, margins might be a little tight for fitting all replies. A separate thread view gives you all the space you need. Cozy!

Open & citable

All public annotations are under the CC-BY license, meaning that they can be cited everywhere while giving proper credit to the author. Impact and attribution maximized!
